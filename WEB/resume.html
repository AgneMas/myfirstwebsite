About me

I am an Artist from Lithuania based in Berlin. I graduated Photography and Media Art in Vilnius Art Academy. I am interested in concepts based on our culture and environments. I seek to document how the perception of our images changes with the development of technology and the mixing of the physical and virtual worlds
